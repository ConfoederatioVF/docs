Abstract

Confoederatio Element (https://element.confoederatio.org/) is the self-hosted instance of Matrix Element used for important communications. It bridges the main Confoederatio Discord, meaning messages within CONFOEDERATIO_ travel back and forth between multiple chat services.

Hosting for servers are kept on hardware (8TB, 128GB RAM) maintained by Confoederatio members with physical ownership. It is not a VPS. Traffic is routed via Cloudflare tunnels to Confoederatio's main domain, which is why the subdomain is element. As a Matrix instance, the federated URL is matrix.confoederatio.org.

Email verification is managed via Resend/Proton, so you will see emails from noreply@confoederatio.org when verifying. Domain hosting is hybridised between Cloudflare/GoDaddy in case of an outage on either. Users can create custom Spaces/Rooms which may either be public or E2EE for confidential messaging. Only the primary CONFOEDERATIO_ space is federated. Phone verification is also available as a secondary option, though we understand that not all members may wish to verify this way.

The .onion domain for Confoederatio Element connects directly to our physical servers, without Cloudflare tunnels or other proxies, but you must use Tor or another browser with which it is compatible: http://rrqaziswwxa2kuv5qxiyuwfhr6u6p3ohly3ig7lmgrve3juyyhcvq2yd.onion/.

Migrating to Confoederatio Element

Members, associates, or others with the public link may also feel free to migrate their Discords to Confoederatio Element. We provide technical services on how to do so, and you can register a bridge between your Confoederatio Element Space and your primary Discord server via https://bridge.confoederatio.org/. This will require you to have the bot invited with read/write perms in the channels you wish to link.

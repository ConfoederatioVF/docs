Collation is our OSINT suite for geospatialised news aggregation and tracking live events and ORBATs. Because its data pipelines are currently being consolidated, it requires local self-hosting and is mainly intended for developer use at its current stage. It also conveniently functions as the main dev branch for Naissance.

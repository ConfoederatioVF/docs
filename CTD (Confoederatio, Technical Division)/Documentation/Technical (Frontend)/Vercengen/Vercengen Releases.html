Vercengen Releases

This is a list of all current Vercengen releases up to 10 June 2026, and will be periodically updated as available. Versions prior to 0.5b Lomekwi were released under the name BrowserUI, see BrowserUI Releases.

Version names were chosen to relate to human technological eras as a form of shorthand.
